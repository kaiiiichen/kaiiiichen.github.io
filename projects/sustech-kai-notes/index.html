Launched in 2025, SUSTech-Kai-Notes is a personal notes initiative motivated by Cambridge Notes. However, one should also lay emphasis on other course resources apart from course notes.

It mainly includes the course resources I ever used or collected during my study at SUSTech and also includes few courses I took at other universities, which are mostly for general study credits but worth giving it a try.

I accompany this special notes project with a Github wiki page, which reviews the project and helps you get directed to submodules within clicks.
